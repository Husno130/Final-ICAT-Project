Cancer Awareness

Early detection saves lives. Learn about prevention, symptoms, and screenings.

What is Cancer?

Cancer is a disease caused by uncontrolled cell growth, leading to tumors or abnormal cell function. Early detection can significantly improve treatment outcomes.

Early Detection Matters

Screenings and self-examinations are crucial in identifying cancer in its early stages. Regular checkups can help diagnose cancer before symptoms appear.

Risk Factors

Factors like smoking, unhealthy diets, prolonged sun exposure, and genetics can increase the risk of cancer. Awareness and lifestyle changes can lower these risks.

Prevention and Lifestyle Tips

Adopting a healthy lifestyle can reduce cancer risks. A balanced diet, exercise, avoiding tobacco, and regular screenings play a crucial role in prevention.
